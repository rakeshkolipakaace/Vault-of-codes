Techie Repair

Techie Repair is a web platform that connects customers, technicians, and product sellers for efficient appliance repairs. Customers can easily submit repair requests, and technicians promptly receive them for quick assessment. Once the issue is diagnosed, technicians can communicate with product sellers to check part availability, compare prices, and place orders seamlessly. This streamlined process saves time, enhances communication, and ensures faster repairs, making Techie Repair a convenient hub for all users.
